National Center for Supercomputing Applications Identity Provider

The NCSA Identity Provider (IdP) releases the following attributes to all Service Providers (SP).
  • eduPersonPrincipalName
  • eduPersonTargetedID
  • displayName
  • givenName
  • sn (surName)
  • mail
  • eduPersonScopedAffiliation
See the Incommon Federation Attribute Summary for detailed information.
The NCSA IdP uses Duo for authentication, for which the Duo Services Privacy Notice applies.

Contact help+idp@ncsa.illinois.edu for assistance.
